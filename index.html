Our Facilities

Cardio Zone

Equipped with state-of-the-art treadmills, cycles, and ellipticals for endurance training. Our cardio zone features the latest machines with built-in heart rate monitors and entertainment systems to keep you motivated during your workout.

Strength Area

Dedicated space with free weights, squat racks, and resistance machines. Our strength area is designed for both beginners and advanced lifters, featuring Olympic weightlifting platforms, dumbbells up to 100kg, and a wide range of strength training equipment.

Stretch & Recovery

A calm zone for stretching, foam rolling, and recovery exercises. Our recovery area includes massage therapy stations, foam rollers, stretching mats, and guided recovery sessions to help you prevent injuries and improve flexibility.
